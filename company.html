About Company?

Packaging is the science, art and technology of enclosing or protecting products for distribution, storage, sale, and use. This is how the Wiki definition of Packaging reads. Indeed an art it is. There are many players in the Indian Packaging Machines manufacturing industry, especially in the Vertical Form Fill Seal machine segment. These include both orgainzed and unorganized players. Among these scores of manufacurers there exist a fine breed which have a very balanced, consistent and experienced approach to manufacturing of these essentially technology driven Capital Products. On my recent interviews with some of these companies I got valuable insights. The Faridabad based PackiTTM has been into the business for two decades. It is a key player for the fast evolving and demanding Vertical Form Fill Seal type machines. Making machines with upto 20 head fast track performers, their impressive product line includes cup fillers and auger fillers as well.

The Vertical form fill sealing machine (VFFS) is a type of automated assembly-line product packaging system, commonly used to in thepackaging industry for food, and a wide variety of other products. The machine constructs plastic bags out of a flat roll of plastic film, while simultaneously filling the bags with product and sealing the filled bags. Both solids and liquids can be bagged using this packaging system.PackiTTM range of machines include baggers that are 100% manufactured at the company’s Faridabad Plant. Their latest offering is the Komet81, a high speed multirole bagger.

“Our whole effort is to create best possible value for the buyer, creating excellent RoI for our clients”

says Mr. Subramaniam the GM of the company.

Buyer can engage in communication with the company and let them know their requirements including detailes of required technical capabilities, labor availability, worker safety concerns, maintainability, serviceability, reliability, ability to integrate into the packaging line, capital cost, floorspace, flexibility (change-over, materials, etc.), energy usage,quality of outgoing packages, qualifications (for food, pharmaceuticals, etc.), throughput, efficiency, productivity, ergonomics, return on investment, etc. The company evaluates these requirements and comes with the suggestion of the machine(s) best suited. PackiT TM machines serve packaging requirements of gel, iquid, tablets, power, candies, wafers etc offering packaging by pieces, volume or weight.

PackiT TM machines use top of the line products and spares. These are PLC based machines that can be easily programmed. The PLCs are armored for severe conditions (such as dust, moisture, heat, cold) and have the facility for extensive input/output (I/O) arrangements.

The new comperehensive range of PackiTTM Vertical Form Fill Seal Machines are now available with servo driven sealing systems to give increased packaging performances at high speeds.Maximum efficiencies are achieved on a large range of products and bag types such as pillow, pouch, stand up. The PLC operated machines feature Auto film tracking, Film splicing unit, Power film unwind system, low level film alarm and encoder or photocell measurement. Available directly from the company or dealerships as well, the product availability and service is ensured throughout India and across global locations as well.
